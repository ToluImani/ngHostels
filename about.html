About Us

About Us

ngHostels is a small startup founded in August 2017 and based in Abeokuta, its a marketplace where students meet homeowners and rent off-campus hostels at different universities in Nigeria- all from the comfort of the internet with any internet-enabled device.


Whether its low-budget accommodation or high-brow student apartments that you're looking for, you'll be sure to find it listed here.


ngHostels is the easiest way to rent your next student accommodation,we offer the lowest prices and all hostels listed on our platform has been checked by our agents for quality and comfort.

Currently we operate in the following Higher Institutions:


Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta.

University of Ibadan.

Federal College of Education, Osiele.

Polytechnic Ibadan.

Moshood Abiola Polytechnic, Abeokuta.


As an expanding company we intend to cover more Institutions and offer more services soon.
